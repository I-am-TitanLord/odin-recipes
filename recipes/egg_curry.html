Egg Curry

Description

Egg Curry is a deliciously rich onion tomato gravy, tastes exactly like the ones you get in dhabas. These are perfect with some rice or paratha and fresh salad on the side. Step by step video included after the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Onion, Tomato
  • Tempering : Consists of
    • Ginger
    • Garlic
    • Green Chilies
  • Spices
  • Fresh Cream

Recipe

  1. Boil 4-6 eggs, peel, and set aside.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and sauté 1 tsp cumin seeds until fragrant.
  3. Add 2 finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste and cook until aromatic.
  5. Add 2 chopped tomatoes, 1/4 tsp turmeric, and 1/2 tsp red chili powder; cook until soft.
  6. Add 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala, and salt to taste; mix well.
  7. Add 1.5 cups water and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Make small slits in the boiled eggs, add to the gravy, and coat with spices.
  9. Simmer for 5 minutes, letting the eggs absorb the flavors.
  10. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
